About me

I was born in China and came here to States when I was 17. After my high school in Philadelphia, I moved to Seattle and got my Bachelor degree at University of Washington in major of Visual Art. I am now studying at ASU as a graduate student and majoring in Visual Communicate Design. Hope you guys enjoy my works and welcome to join me!

Studio 590

I took GRA590 at ASU during summer 2019. It is a studio class to experience some fundamental ideas and skills of graphic design. Here are some projects about forms, shapes, fonts and animation. Most of my projects in this class were made by Indesign, and animation project was made by After Effects. It is a great opportunity for me to practice using these software.

Lean In

• Bookcover design •

The user group of this book is for Chinese customers who want to read the original English version, so the cover design has Chinese characters and seal elements. I chose the deer as the main background, because the deer seems to be a very gentle animal, but her horns and her looks very fortitude, more in line with the image of the author.

Bites

• Brand/logo/package design •

This brand design of chewy treats was inspired by my golden retriever Pepper. Pepper is still a baby boy in his molar period. A thought of designing a special, concise, clear and healthy brand of dog chews was flashed in my mind when I was looking for molar products for him.

Yumso

• Logo/UI design •

Chinese people living abroad can taste Chinese food and promote Chinese food culture together. It is a convenient food delivery service for Chinese food lovers who are far away from abroad to order food and shop location.
